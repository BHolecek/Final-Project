Sports

The Kansas City Chiefs have just concluded their 2020 NFL Draft. Among the six draft picks, is a running back, linebacker, offensive lineman, defensive end and two cornerbacks. They are also returning 20 of their 22 starters from this past season's Super Bowl winning team. That is near historic levels of teams retaining their core following a Super Bowl win.

For more on the 2020 Kansas City Chiefs draft picks, they list the names of Clyde Edwards-Helaire, Willie Gay Jr., Lucas Niang, L'Jarius Sneed, Michael Danna and BoPete Keys at the top of the home page. From there, you can click on a link to learn more about the draftees. There is a ton of material to read and watch once you learn more about these players.
